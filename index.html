Who is Lebron James?

Lebron Raymone James Sr. is arguably the greatest basketball player of all time, currently playing for the Los Angeles Lakers. Nicknamed "King James", he is NBA's all time leading scorer and has won 4 times NBA championship from 10 NBA final appearances. He also played for the Cavaliers and Heat.

LeBron's backstory

  • Born on December 30th, 1984, in Akron, Ohio
  • Grew up with his single mother in poverty
  • Introduced to basketball at a young age
  • Played really well in high school
  • Drafted to Cleveland Cavaliers in 2003
